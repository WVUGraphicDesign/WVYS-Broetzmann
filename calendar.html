The WVYS follows the Kanawha County School schedule.

weather, rehearsal will be cancelled. Additional performance
opportunities are anticipated as the year progresses; however,
dates are not yet confirmed. When time permits, schedule
changes will be announced through the newsletter. Please
check email and the web site for last minute changes.
