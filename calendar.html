Calendar

Please Note! The following schedule is subject to change. The WVYS follows the Kanawha County School schedule. If schools are closed or dismissed early due to inclement weather, rehearsal will be cancelled. Additional performance opportunities are anticipated as the year progresses; however, dates are not yet confirmed. When time permits, schedule changes will be announced through the newsletter. Please check email and the web site for last minute changes.

Rehearsal begins promptly at 6:30pm. Plan to arrive early to unpack your instrument and tune. You should be in your seat ready to play at 6:30pm.
